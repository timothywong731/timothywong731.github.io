Timothy (Tim) is an AI/ML professional with over ten years of experience in big data, data science, and analytics applications. His experience spans multiple sectors, including energy, telecommunications, defense and national security. He has a strong, proven track record of delivering complex AI/ML applications in enterprise environments and has led teams of experienced engineers. Tim is also a published researcher in ML and a frequent conference speaker. He is professionally qualified as both a Chartered Statistician (CStat) and a Chartered Engineer (CEng).
